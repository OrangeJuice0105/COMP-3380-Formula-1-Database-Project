## Supported SQL queries

As of our now (April 5, 2026), we are currently supporting the following queries:

- **Drivers Performance by Season**: Retrieves the list of every drivers for each season and displays their names, races participated, number of wins and podiums, total points and average finishing position for that season.
- **Constructors with most DNFs by season**: Gets all seasons and display the constructor with the most amount of DNFs for each season.
- **Hat-trick List**: Gets all race events where a *hat-trick* was achieved (i.e., obtained pole position, fastest lap and win in the same event) and the driver's name who achieved this feat.
- **Grand Slam List**: Gets all race events where a *Grand slam* was achieved (i.e., obtained pole position, fastest lap and win plus led every race laps in the same event) and the driver's name who achieved this feat.
- **Lap degradation**: Compares lap times across a stint or race distance to show how lap pace worsens over time due to tyre wear, fuel load changes, and race conditions.
- **Most championship points in a season**: Gets top 10 drivers and seasons where they achieved the highest points tally.
- **Most consecutive driver wins**: Gets top 10 drivers who has the most consecutive race wins along with the number of races won, the race event where the streak started and the race event where the streak ended.
- **Drivers with most DNFs per season**: Retrieves every season and displays all drivers who had most races ended with a DNF for the season.
- **Most wins but not champions**: Gets the seasons and the respective driver each season who achieved the most race victories but ultimately did not win the championship.
- **Races for a single constructor where no drivers are classified**: Gets the race events for a given constructor that both of their drivers were not classified (either due to not starting the race or sufferred from a DNF).
- **Races where all drivers are classified**: Gets all race events that every driver is classified (either finished the race or DNFed but still classified due to having completed 90% of the race winner's distance).
- **Wins without pole**: Gets top 10 drivers who achieved the most amount of race wins without starting from pole position.
- **Worst pole-to-win ratio**: Gets top 10 drivers who have the lowest pole-to-win ratio (i.e., rate of converting a pole position start to a race victory).
- **Fastest average pit stop team by season**: Retrieves the constructor with the fastest average pit stop time for each season.
- **Constructor teammate head-to-head**: Gets a head-to-head results from all constructors for all seasons. This head-to-head only takes final race finishing position into consideration.
- **List drivers**: Gets all drivers who have participated in at least one session.
- **List constructors**: Gets all constructors who have participated.
- **List circuits**: Gets all the circuits that have hosted at least one *Formula 1* race event in history.
